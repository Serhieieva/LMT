Today digital knowledge acquisition does not keep up with technological capabilities. Reading and learning online is still time-intensive and effortful.

Most platforms only provide superficial information and hardly any background. Contextualization of data still has a lot of untapped potential.

We do more than inform. We educate our readers on the topics that matter. We call this focus: "infocation"

Hence, our platforms are not solely aggregating high quality news, but are adding further value through deep personalization features and a unique contextualization of information.

Users save a lot of time and research efforts by using our solutions. They enable them to see the whole angle on important matters and to improve their decision-making in both private and business life.

We would love to see you using our products and supporting our company.

We create powerful web technologies that faciliate digital knowledge acquisition.

We strive to become the largest digital knowledge network worldwide and the go-to place for personalized and contextualized information.

  • PERSONALIZATION

    We provide granular personalization features, giving users freedom to choose what they wish to consume in the fashion they want to consume it.

  • CONTEXT

    We provide context to deep dive into topics instead of superficial media consumption. This enables our readers to save a lot of time and effort as well as improve their decision-making.

  • TRANSPARENCY

    We provide transparent information about our use of data, our actions and reasons for those. We believe trust is crucial in a partnership between us and our users.

Knowlege in Context

News Spectator is an "infocation" aggregator that assembles political contents from public institutions. Users can personalize news based on their individual political interests on six different profiles.

Additionally, all news are mechanically contextualized, i.e. complemented by relevant background information such as facts on institutions and protagonists, important charts and statistics, relevant non-fiction books and more.

The News Spectator brings together information and education services to fully grasp the whole context of a topic. It thus reduces time and research efforts for its target group, people working or interested in the political field such as journalists, politicians, associations, officials and alike.
